Butter Chicken Recipie



Description

This is a recipie of the butter chicken dish from the Pakistani Tradition. It is a mix of creamy and spicy chicken.

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Heavy Cream
  • Tomato Puree
  • Spices

Steps

  1. Cut the chicken
  2. Sautee the chicken
  3. Add the heavy cream and tomato puree
  4. Plate the chicken
